carbon.txt is a proposed convention to verify that IT infrastucture uses green power.

If a site or digital services supports carbon.txt, you'll be able to trace a path from the site to where the energy it uses comes from, and the steps taken to stop it contributing to climate change.

It does this with governance structures we aleady use - combining the domain name system on the internet, and existing standards and conventions for accounting for carbon emissions from generating electricity used in energy markets today.
